The Three Structural Forces Audit

Every business is a bridge. Revenue flow, hidden leverage, and relationship connections hold it up — and silently crack it. This audit is the same map I use to expose liabilities. Answer the stress tests below, and I’ll show you which pillar is most fragile.

1. Revenue Flow Stress Test

If your three largest clients paid 30 days late, what would happen? Would you miss payroll? Break a supplier agreement? Write it down — the answer is your cash‑gap fracture.

2. Tension‑Based Leverage Stress Test

Calculate your fixed monthly nut (rent, salaries, software you can’t cancel quickly). Divide it by your average monthly revenue. If that number is over 60% and you have less than a month of cash, you are structurally tense.

Quick diagnostic:

3. Relationship Connection Stress Test

What percentage of your total revenue comes from your single biggest customer? If it’s over 30%, you are structurally dependent on a single bolt.
