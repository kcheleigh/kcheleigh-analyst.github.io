Process Blind Spot Self-Check | Khiva Cheleigh Business Topology Analyst

90 seconds. 5 questions. Uncover one invisible bottleneck hiding in your operations.

1. Does work routinely stall for more than 4 hours between handoffs (waiting for approval, clarification, or someone’s availability)?

2. Are there one or two people who constantly get pulled into decisions or escalations, even if they aren’t formally accountable?

3. Do you suspect critical steps are being done in spreadsheets, shadow systems, or manual workarounds that nobody officially tracks?

4. If your main revenue process were a landscape, would you say there are “swamps” where items get stuck or disappear?

5. Can you confidently state, right now, what percentage of your end‑to‑end lead time is pure waiting (not actual work)?
