Process Quick Check

Check the statements that apply to your business. A quick score reveals if a deeper topology scan is worth your time.

1. We’ve had customer complaints about slow delivery or inconsistent service in the last 3 months.

2. Decisions that should take hours routinely take days because they get stuck waiting for one person.

3. We rely on spreadsheets, emails, or informal chats to track key operational steps.

4. When something goes wrong, it’s hard to trace who was responsible or where it broke.

5. We’ve hired or are hiring an ops manager because things are falling through the cracks.
